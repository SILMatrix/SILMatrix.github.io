Software Implementation Lifecycle Matrix (SILM)

  • SILM emerged as logic instrument for Partners to reconcile their interests in the process of software implementation. SILM is elegantly intuitive for experts and beginners.
  • SILM is a system of Concepts. SILM does not impose specific Methods on Partners. SILM systemized Concepts in each of 99 Cells. Concept can be drilled down to description of specific Methods and Best Practice.
  • SILM is 3-D Matrix based on 10 hierarchical Levels.
  • SILM is based on decimal Process Pattern
